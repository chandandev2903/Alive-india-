Alive India In Concert

The heart of Alive

language Website play_circle_filled Youtube photo_camera Instagram facebook Facebook

Recognized as India's largest and longest-running live music concert series, Alive India in concert is an expanding 10-season legacy across Multiple Indian Cities partnering with India's most Admired Artists, Venues and Brands.

What sets it apart is its unprecedented pre-concert activation, engaging music lovers for 2-3 months before each concert.

This interactive platform also unites leading brands, creating unique experiences for all with a mix of grand and intimate shows.

We live for the cheering sound of our audience. The magic of a open air concert is hard to put in words. So Instead, We invite you to attend and experience it.
