The Visionary Behind ALIVE

SUPRATIEK

GHOSH

The dynamic founder of ALIVE, is a trailblazing musician, entrepreneur, and visionary, recipient of Music Excellence Award from Vice President of India for his contribution to Indian Independent Music. With over 30 years of Media + Music industry experience, he is an acclaimed Singer of T Series, Times Music.

In 2000, Supratik formed his band, Aurko, with a mission to promote "Indian Music, Indian Dreams". Aurko took flight. Signed by Times Music, they released three albums with 24 original songs, touring extensively across India and abroad for 19 years.

With a deep understanding of the music landscape, Supratik is leading ALIVE on a remarkable journey, paving the way for a brighter future for Indian music.
